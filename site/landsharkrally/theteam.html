The team:

in front, from left to right:
Alex Slepak was born in Russia, and the command of the Russian language was occasionally helpful while being continuously lost over the course of the 2006 Mongol Rally as part of team RustBox.360. On other occasions it was admittedly a detriment - the hope is that the language and the experience will somehow help this time around too... Tentatively in charge of navigation

Dave Lott has hiked/climbed/explored every hill, mountain, crevasse, etc. in the state of Washington over the past 12 months. So, he's officially in charge of all camping and hiking up the various tall mountains that we'll be encountering along the way. This was kinda his idea too, in some variation.

Evelyn Spence is a a free-lance writer, specializing in the outdoors, winter sports, and exotic locales in general. She will, therefore, be in charge of making the stories of our lost adventures seem readable, and hopefully even interesting and exciting!
