What in the world are we up to?
The world's a big place, and surprisingly enough, you can cover most of it in a car! The Mongol Rally tracks across most of Europe and Asia. The Pan-American highway traverses North and South America. The Dakar Rally crosses the Sahara desert and Northern Africa. So a brilliant idea was born - why not combine all of them and drive a 25-yr old car 60,000 miles all the way around the world?
